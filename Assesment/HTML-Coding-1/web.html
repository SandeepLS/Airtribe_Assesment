My Favorite Hobby

Why I Love It

I love photography because it allows to me capture & cherish moments that would otherwise be last in time.
It gives me a creative outlet & express my perspective of the world helps me to see beauty in everyday life.
Photography also offers endless learning opportunities, from mastering technical skills to developing an artistic eye.

How to Get Started

  1. Choose a camera that fits your budget and needs.
  2. Learn the basic settings: ISO, shutter speed, and aperture.
  3. Practice shooting in different lighting conditions.
  4. Experiment with composition and framing.
  5. Edit your photos using software like Lightroom or Photoshop.
